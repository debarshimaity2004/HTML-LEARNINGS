Workouts to Perform

Warm-Up (5–10 min)

Prepare your body and reduce injury risk.

  • Jumping jacks
  • Arm circles
  • Light jogging or brisk walking
  • Dynamic stretches (leg swings, torso twists)

Strength Training

Build muscle and increase strength.

  • Push-ups (chest, arms)
  • Squats (legs, glutes)
  • Lunges (legs, balance)
  • Plank (core stability)
  • Dumbbell rows (back)

Cardio Workouts

Improve heart health and burn calories.

  • Running or jogging
  • Cycling
  • Skipping rope
  • Burpees
  • Mountain climbers

Flexibility & Cool-Down (5–10 min)

Relax muscles and aid recovery.

  • Hamstring stretch
  • Quad stretch
  • Shoulder and arm stretch
  • Child’s pose (yoga)
  • Deep breathing exercises
