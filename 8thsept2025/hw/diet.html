Pre & Post Workout Diet

Pre-Workout Diet

Eat foods that give you energy and prevent fatigue.

  • Carbohydrates: main source of energy (e.g., oats, banana, rice).
  • Protein: helps protect muscles (e.g., yogurt, milk, eggs).
  • Fat & Fiber: keep low just before workout to avoid stomach issues.

Examples: Chicken with rice (2–3 hrs before), oats with banana (1–2 hrs before), or a small fruit (30 min before).

Post-Workout Diet

Eat foods that help muscles recover and restore energy.

  • Carbohydrates: refuel energy stores (e.g., rice, sweet potato, fruits).
  • Protein: repairs muscles (e.g., whey shake, paneer, fish, lentils).
  • Hydration: drink water and add electrolytes if needed.

Examples: Whey shake with banana (within 1 hr), salmon with sweet potato (1–2 hrs), or rice bowl with tofu (2–4 hrs).
